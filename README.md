> **21 million Americans** have a substance use disorder. Only **10% receive treatment.** The gap between crisis and help is where people die. And the stigma keeps millions from even asking.

## The Solution

6 agents with zero-judgment design. We don't ask why. We ask how we can help. MANDATORY SAMHSA handoff for active crisis.

## Agents

**CrisisResponder** — Immediate intervention, MANDATORY SAMHSA 1-800-662-4357
**RecoveryNavigator** — Treatment center finder, insurance verification, bed availability
**SoberCoach** — Daily check-ins, trigger identification, coping strategies
**PeerConnector** — Recovery mentor matching, AA/NA meeting finder
**FamilySupport** — Al-Anon meetings, codependency resources, family education
**RelapsePrevention** — Early warning detection, emergency contacts, safety planning

## Safety

**MANDATORY: SAMHSA National Helpline — 1-800-662-4357.** Active crisis = immediate handoff. Cannot be disabled.

## Data Sources

SAMHSA treatment locator, state licensing databases, meeting finder APIs
